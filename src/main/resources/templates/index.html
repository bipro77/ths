Telemedicine and Healthcare Service

Project Description: Nowadays telemedicine is an important aspect of healthcare services. For the increasing need for diagnosis, treatment, and disease prevention of various patients, telemedicine service serves patients remotely. In this project, a website will be build where patients will take medical service from highly qualified doctors. Here, doctors and patients can get service after a successful registration. Both of them can communicate through this website for further analysis of the disease. Patients can choose which doctor will be suitable for them and make an appointment.
